About this site

I made this site almost entirely on an iPad while commuting on the Tube. It's built with Jekyll and Bootstrap, stored in Bitbucket and hosted by Netlify. I used Graphic, Affinity Designer and Affinity Photo for graphics, Working Copy for coding and Bear for writing.
