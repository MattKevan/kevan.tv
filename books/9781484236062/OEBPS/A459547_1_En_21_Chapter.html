21. It’s Not Cheating

James Brett1 

(1)Forest Lodge, New South Wales, Australia

You don’t get any points in life for doing things the hard way.

—Tim Fargo, entrepreneur, author and investor1

It’s not cheating to have a set of quick reference materials that you can use—either electronically on your devices or printed versions that can be displayed as big visual charts around your space. For most of us, visuals are a really powerful tool to assist our understanding and recall of information. Having visuals displayed prominently in our environment keeps the content front of mind and helps us break old habits and drive our evolution. I highly recommend you find at least one visual that resonates with you and display it where you will see it regularly throughout your day.
